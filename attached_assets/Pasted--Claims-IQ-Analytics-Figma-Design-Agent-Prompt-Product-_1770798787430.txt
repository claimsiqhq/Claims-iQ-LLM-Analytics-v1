## 1. Product Context

Claims IQ Analytics is a **chat-first analytics interface** for claims operations. There are no traditional dashboards â€” the conversation IS the product. Users (primarily Claims Managers) ask questions in natural language, receive auto-generated charts and insight summaries, and refine their analysis through threaded dialogue. Dashboards "emerge" organically from pinned conversation threads over time.

The design philosophy is **data density over decoration**, **progressive disclosure**, and **calm confidence**. The interface should feel like a sharp, trustworthy analyst sitting beside you â€” not a flashy BI tool.

## 13. Design Principles (Summary)

1. **Data density over decoration.** Maximize information per pixel. No ornamental chrome.
2. **Progressive disclosure.** Essential view first â€” details on interaction (hover, click, expand).
3. **Calm confidence.** The purple/gold palette conveys authority and trust. Red is reserved exclusively for genuine alerts (SLA breaches, errors, stale data).
4. **Spatial consistency.** Chart always in the same place. Metadata always below. Insight always above. No layout shifts between states.
5. **Human + Intelligent.** The interface empowers the Claims Manager â€” it does not overwhelm or replace them. Tone is professional, concise, and helpful.
6. **Radical transparency.** Every chart shows its assumptions, filters, time range, data freshness, and parsed intent. Nothing is a black box.
